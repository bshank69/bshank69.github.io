About This Project

In today's digital era, creativity resides within each of us, yet many often struggle to bring that creativity to life. While this website may appear simple, it serves as a testament to how artificial intelligence can empower individuals who may lack traditional technical skills to freely express their ideas. This project was entirely built using ChatGPT, with no code written by me personally. My intention is to showcase the potential of AI as a powerful tool, demonstrating both its capability and the exciting possibilities it offers in creative endeavors.
